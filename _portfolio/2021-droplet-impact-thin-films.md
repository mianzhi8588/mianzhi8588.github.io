## Overview

This project investigated the dynamics of liquid droplet impact on thin films, focusing on how different impact heights and liquid-film thicknesses affect droplet spreading, crown formation, and morphological evolution.

## My Role

**Project Leader**

## My Contributions

- Designed and built a droplet collision test rig based on prior research designs.
- Used a calibration plate and high-speed camera to capture droplet impact images.
- Processed high-speed images to identify droplet morphology and extract characteristic frame-level information.
- Conducted non-dimensional analysis of the acquired data.
- Derived empirical formulas to describe morphological changes during the droplet impingement process.
- Analyzed working-condition data under different impact heights and liquid-film thicknesses.
- Summarized the full research process in a research paper.
