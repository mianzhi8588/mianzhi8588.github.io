## Overview

I participated in a faculty-led academic exchange program in Singapore, engaging with academic discussions at NUS and NTU on technological innovation systems, AI governance, and social management.

## Themes

- Technological innovation systems
- AI governance
- Social management
- Institutional frameworks for technology adoption

## Reflection

This experience broadened my understanding of how innovation policy, data governance, and institutional environments shape the adoption of emerging technologies in public and private sectors.
