## Overview

I served as a science instructor at Beijing Science Center, where I delivered STEM workshops for primary school students and helped organize interactive science activities.

## Role and Contribution

- Delivered hands-on STEM workshops for young students.
- Organized interactive science activities to foster curiosity in mechanical engineering and scientific exploration.
- Practiced communicating engineering concepts to non-specialist audiences.

## Reflection

This outreach experience strengthened my ability to explain technical ideas in accessible language and reinforced my interest in using engineering education to broaden students' exposure to science and technology.
