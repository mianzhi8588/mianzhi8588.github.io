This paper proposes a domain adaptation framework with joint distribution alignment adversarial learning for open-set bearing intelligent fault diagnosis. The study addresses cross-domain fault diagnosis scenarios where the target domain may contain unknown fault categories, improving the robustness and generalization ability of bearing fault diagnosis models under open-set conditions.
